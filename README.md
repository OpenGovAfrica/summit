### OpenGov Africa Summit 2026  
#### Building Africa’s Most Open and Participatory Continental Convening

The OpenGov Africa Summit 2026 is a multi-sector, multi-region convergence designed to bridge the gap between grassroots innovation and high-level policy across Africa.

Taking place as a Global Hybrid Experience, the summit combines a high-level physical hub in Lagos, Nigeria with a fully integrated virtual platform, enabling participation from within the continent and across the globe.

### A New Model for African Convenings

Historically, many high-level policy and technology conversations about Africa have been:
- geographically limited  
- institutionally closed  
- disconnected from grassroots realities  

The OpenGov Africa Summit is designed to change that model.

We are building a convening that is:
- structurally inclusive  
- regionally representative  
- operationally transparent  
- globally accessible  

### The Open Summit Approach

#### Open Participation  
Through a public Call for Proposals, we are democratizing access to the stage. Alongside invited government officials, CEOs, and industry leaders, we are intentionally creating space for emerging voices, builders, researchers, and community actors.

#### Open Access  
All sessions are free to attend. The virtual summit ensures that geography, cost, and institutional affiliation are not barriers to participation.

#### Open Representation  
The summit is structured to reflect the full diversity of the continent. We strongly encourage participation from women, youth, persons with disabilities, informal sector leaders, and underrepresented communities.

#### Open Collaboration  
This is not a one-way conference. It is a collaborative platform where ideas are shared, challenged, and developed across sectors and regions.

### A Truly Hybrid, Continental Experience

To ensure both depth and scale, the summit operates across two synchronized environments:

#### The Physical Hub  
A high-impact, curated environment in Lagos hosting:
- keynote plenaries  
- executive roundtables  
- closed-door strategic conversations  
- high-value networking  

#### The Global Virtual Summit  
A parallel digital experience featuring:
- multi-track sessions  
- interactive panels and workshops  
- virtual networking spaces  
- community-driven programming  

This dual structure ensures both exclusivity where necessary and openness where it matters most.

### From Event to Ecosystem

The OpenGov Africa Summit is not designed as a one-time gathering.

It is the foundation of an ongoing ecosystem that:
- connects stakeholders across sectors  
- surfaces new ideas and talent  
- produces actionable insights  
- supports long-term collaboration  

Through open calls, community participation, and post-summit outputs, the impact of the summit extends far beyond the event dates.

### Our Commitment to Equity and Inclusion

We recognize that Africa’s strength lies in its diversity.

We are intentional about ensuring that the voices shaping conversations at this summit reflect the realities of the continent. This includes active efforts to elevate:
- women and youth leaders  
- grassroots innovators  
- persons with disabilities  
- representatives from underserved and informal sectors  

Inclusion is not a statement. It is a design principle.

### Organized by the Community, for the Continent

The OpenGov Africa Summit is powered by a distributed volunteer community.

This community-led model ensures that:
- the summit remains grounded in real African contexts  
- participation is not restricted only to elite networks  
- ownership is shared across regions  

All sessions are open and free, reinforcing our commitment to accessibility and public value.
