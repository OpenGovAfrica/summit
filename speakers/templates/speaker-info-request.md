### Speaker Info Request Template

Use this after a speaker confirms, to collect everything needed for the schedule page, program, and AV setup in one pass.

**Subject:** A few details we need ahead of your OpenGov Africa Summit session

Dear [Name],

Thank you again for joining us for [Day X, Track Theme] at the OpenGov Africa Summit 2026. To get you into the program and schedule, could you send over the following by [deadline]?

**1. Session details**
- Final talk title
- Abstract (2 to 3 sentences, for the public schedule)
- 3 to 5 keywords or themes your talk touches on

**2. Bio and photo**
- Short bio (75 to 100 words, third person)
- High resolution headshot (JPEG or PNG, at least 500x500px)
- Current title and organization, exactly as you'd like it listed

**3. Slides (if using)**
- [ ] Yes, I'll be using slides
- [ ] No, I will not be using slides

If yes, please send slides by [deadline]. We recommend 8 to 12 slides, visual over text-heavy.

**4. Format preferences**
- Q&A: moderator-relayed questions, or would you prefer to take questions directly?
- [Virtual only] Recording: any specific requirements for your recording setup?
- [In-person only] Travel: has your travel and accommodation been confirmed?

**5. Social and links**
- LinkedIn or preferred professional link, for program and outreach mentions
- Any organizational tags or partner credits you'd like included

Thank you, we're looking forward to your session.

[Sender Name]
OpenGov Africa Summit 2026
